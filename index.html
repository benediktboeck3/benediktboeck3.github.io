Guacamole

For four servings

  • lime (or lemon)
  • 1/2 diced red onion
  • 2 small tomatoes, preferable Cherry tomatoes

Steps

  2. Then remove the skin, you can either peel it or use a spoon. Then put the fruit into your bowl
